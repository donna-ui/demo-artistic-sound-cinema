            STAGE 01 / SHELL
            
The empty room.

            
23 by 16 by 9 feet. 3,312 cubic feet of air. Before a single panel goes up, we measure the geometry, the reflections, the standing waves — and design backwards from the room that needs to exist.

            STAGE 02 / ACOUSTIC TREATMENT
            
25 panels. Tuned.

            
Bass traps in the corners. Broadband absorbers at the first reflection points. Diffusers on the back wall. The room stops fighting itself. HAA-certified room layout.

            
RT60 TARGET 0.34s @ 1 kHz
PANELS 25 (16 absorb · 9 diffuse)
REFLECTIONS CONTROLLED

          

          

            STAGE 03 / SPEAKERS — 7.2.4
            
A speaker for every direction.

            
Left, center, right. Two side surrounds. Two rear surrounds. Four height channels for Atmos. Two subwoofers, calibrated by SPL meter, not by ear. THX reference layout.

            
CONFIG 7.2.4 ATMOS
FREQ. RESPONSE 18Hz – 20kHz ±2dB
SPL TARGET 105dB @ MLP

          

          

            STAGE 04 / IMAGE
            
A screen that vanishes.

            
JVC RS3100 native 4K laser projector. 138″ acoustically transparent screen at a 2.39:1 anamorphic ratio. ISF-calibrated grayscale, primaries, gamma. The black bars disappear. The screen disappears. The film stays.

            
PROJECTOR JVC RS3100 / 4K LASER
SCREEN 138″ · 2.39:1 · GAIN 1.3
OUTPUT 2,800 ANSI · D65

FILE 03 / THE MOAT

        

          
Four certifications. Held together by almost no one.

          
The audio-and-video integration trade has hundreds of installers in California. Less than a handful hold all four of these. Each one represents a different discipline — install, acoustics, calibration, ethics — and Jeff has done the coursework, sat the exams, and stayed current on every one.

        

        

          

            
CEDIA

            
Custom Electronic Design + Installation Assn.

            
The trade body for the entire AV-integration industry. Standards, ethics, continuing education.

          

          

            
THX

            
THX Cinema Standard

            
The cinema acoustic standard founded at Lucasfilm. The certification on the lobby card.

          

          

            
HAA

            
Home Acoustics Alliance

            
Room acoustics and tuning. The discipline that makes the same speakers sound different in different rooms.

          

          

            
ISF

            
Imaging Science Foundation

            
Display calibration. The same body of work Hollywood uses to color-grade theatrical masters.

          

        

        

          FOUR CERTS · ONE INTEGRATOR  ·  ALMOST UNIQUE IN SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA
