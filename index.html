Four certifications. Held by almost no integrator in California.

          
The audio-video integration trade has hundreds of installers in this state. Less than a handful hold all four of these. Each one is a separate discipline — install, acoustics, calibration, ethics — and Jeff has done the coursework, sat the exams, and stayed current on every one since 2008.

        

        

          

            
CEDIA

            
Custom Electronic Design + Installation Assn.

            
The trade body for the entire AV-integration industry. Standards, ethics, continuing education.

          

          

            
THX

            
THX Cinema Standard

            
The cinema acoustic standard founded at Lucasfilm. The certification on the lobby card.

          

          

            
HAA

            
Home Acoustics Alliance

            
Room acoustics. The discipline that makes the same speakers sound different in different rooms.

          

          

            
ISF

            
Imaging Science Foundation

            
Display calibration. The same body of work Hollywood uses to color-grade theatrical masters.

          

        

        

          FOUR CERTS · ONE INTEGRATOR  ·  Tuned, not turned on.
        

      

    


    
    

      

      

        
We don’t mount TVs.

        

          

          

            
Smart home is the layer that turns the lights down. The product is the room. We build cinemas, listening rooms, and outdoor moments — tuned to ISF, THX, and HAA, the same body of work Hollywood uses to color-grade its theatrical masters. The remote is incidental.

            
Not the soundbar tier. Not the Best Buy tier. The tier where the screen disappears, the room takes over, and you forget you’re in your house.

          

        

      

    


    
    

      

        

          
        

        

          

            Stage one. The shell.
            
The empty room.

            
23 by 16 by 9 feet. 3,312 cubic feet of air. Before a single panel goes up, we measure the geometry, the reflections, the standing waves — and design backwards from the room that needs to exist.

            
VOLUME 3,312 ft³
BASE RT60 0.92s @ 1 kHz
STATUS UNTUNED

          

          

            Stage two. Acoustic treatment.
            
25 panels. Tuned.

            
Bass traps in the corners. Broadband absorbers at the first reflection points. Diffusers on the back wall. The room stops fighting itself. HAA-certified room layout.

            
RT60 TARGET 0.34s @ 1 kHz
PANELS 25 (16 absorb · 9 diffuse)
REFLECTIONS CONTROLLED

          

          

            Stage three. Speakers, 7.2.4.
            
A speaker for every direction.

            
Left, center, right. Two side surrounds. Two rear surrounds. Four height channels for Atmos. Two subwoofers, calibrated by SPL meter, not by ear. THX reference layout.

            
CONFIG 7.2.4 ATMOS
FREQ. RESPONSE 18Hz – 20kHz ±2dB
SPL TARGET 105dB @ MLP

          

          

            Stage four. Image.
            
A screen that vanishes.

            
JVC RS3100 native 4K laser projector. 138″ acoustically transparent screen at a 2.39:1 anamorphic ratio. ISF-calibrated grayscale, primaries, gamma. The black bars disappear. The screen disappears. The film stays.

            
PROJECTOR JVC RS3100 / 4K LASER
SCREEN 138″ · 2.39:1 · GAIN 1.3
OUTPUT 2,800 ANSI · D65

          

          

            Stage five. Light and shade.
            
Five scenes. One button.

            
Lutron Ketra full-spectrum tunable LEDs in the cove and recessed cans. Motorized blackout shades on every aperture. Five preset scenes—Day, Reveal, Dim, Feature, House Up— on a Crestron pico the size of a light switch.

            
FIXTURES 12 KETRA · 6 ZONES
SHADES LUTRON SIVOIA · BLACKOUT
CONTROL 1 PICO · 5 SCENES

          

          

            Stage six. Tuned.
            
Eight seats. One sweet spot.

            
Tiered seating with a 7″ riser. Front row at the calibrated mix position. Eighty-seven hours of design and tuning across one room. Calibrated, not configured.

            
SEATING 8 RECLINE · 2 ROWS
RISER 7″
HOURS LOGGED 087
