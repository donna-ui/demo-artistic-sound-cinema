## Creative direction

- **Aesthetic:** "The Reference Room" — the inside of a Dolby Atmos screening room. Lights down. Projector warming. Cinema spec sheet meets projection booth. Dark architectural cinema. References: Dolby Cinema, McIntosh Labs, Steinway Lyngdorf, A24, Christopher Nolan title cards, Bang & Olufsen.
- **Signature moments:**
  1. **Calibration Sequence** — page calibrates to the user's actual device on load. Real numbers (viewport, refresh rate, color gamut, time of day).
  2. **The Reference Room** — sticky-scroll architectural cross-section of a cinema room with 6 layers revealing in sequence (acoustic, speakers, projector, lighting, seating, tuned).
  3. **The Project Ledger** — sequential install log (Project 001 → 047). Mono. Empty state IS the design.
  4. **Page-level letterbox progression** — meta letterbox bars grow as user scrolls deeper, climaxing at "FEATURE PRESENTATION" before CTA.
  5. **Opt-in audio cues** — A 440Hz reference tone on phone tap; service-section ambient tones.
- **Color palette:**
  - Cinema Black `#050505` (60%)
  - Walnut Structural `#1F1813` (15%)
  - Bone Reverse `#E8E2D5` (10%)
  - Projection Amber `#E8A547` (10%)
  - DCI-P3 Cyan `#4FB8E8` (5%)
- **Typography:**
  - Display: **Bodoni Moda 900** (high-contrast didone, no italic) — title-card cinematic
  - Body: **Hanken Grotesk** — technical, geometric, architectural
  - Data: **JetBrains Mono** — calibration readouts, project ledger, mono watermarks
- **Photography:** ONE hero — Pexels photo 13348768 (dark luxury home theater w/ screen). Otherwise typography + motion + cinematic darkness. Negative space carries the rest.
- **Mono watermark on every section:** `// CAL.0014 / UPLAND, CA` style — film-reel countdown leader.
