On the discipline

Four certifications. Each one a separate body of work.

Hundreds of integrators in California. Less than a handful hold all four.

CEDIA
Custom Electronic Design + Installation Association

The trade body for the entire AV-integration industry. Standards, ethics, continuing education. The professional baseline — the rest is what you do after.

THX
THX Cinema Standard

The cinema acoustic standard founded at Lucasfilm in 1983. The certification on the lobby card. It governs how a room reproduces theatrical sound — channel placement, calibration tolerances, dynamic range.

HAA
Home Acoustics Alliance

Room acoustics and tuning. The discipline that makes the same speakers sound different in different rooms. Bass traps, broadband absorbers, diffusers, decay times. Most installers never sit this exam.

ISF
Imaging Science Foundation

Display calibration. Grayscale, primaries, gamma, color volume. The same body of work Hollywood uses to color-grade theatrical masters. An ISF-calibrated screen reproduces what the director saw on the reference monitor.
